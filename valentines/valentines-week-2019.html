According to me, it doesn't matter whether you are going to propose a girl or boy. What the matter is, how beautifully you express your feelings to your partner. The way of expressing your feelings may differ from person to person. Everyone has it's own way. The most important is before proposing your crush or love you will have to understand his/her very well. You will have to figure out likes or dislikes of your crush/love. You can propose in crowd or in privacy also. SO I think first you need to understand where he/she will feel special? Whether in crowd or Privacy places? So you should opt for the option that will make your crush/love feel special and comfortable.

Best way to propse your crush/love:

  • Went down into your knee.
  • Hold your partner's hand/fingers gently.
  • Express your feeling to his/her in romantic way.
  • Finally offer his/her a beautiful rose and propose him/her.

I hope you will receive positive response.
